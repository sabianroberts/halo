Make It Before the Sunset Campaign

The Make It Before the Sunset campaign is to get the original Xbox 360 Halo games back in their prime before the matchmaking servers shut down forever. The people behind this campaign are aware of the technically superior Master Chief Collection, however we believe that the originals deserve a final run before they are put to rest.
