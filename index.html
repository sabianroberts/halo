THE CAMPAIGN TO GET THE XBOX 360 HALO GAMES BACK INTO THEIR PRIME BEFORE WE SAY GOODBYE

Our Discord Server

Download the Poster

In December 2021, 343 Industries are closing the online matchmaking servers for all the Halo games on the Xbox 360, including Halo 3, Halo 3: ODST, Halo: Reach & Halo: CE Anniversary and Halo 4. It is saddening how little players there are on all games even after 343's announcement back in December 2020; so let's get these games alive again! There's not much time. We only have ten months and Bungie have already closed halo.bungie.net. So why not dust off your old copy of Halo 3, or even your old Xbox 360 (the Xbox 360 Halo games are backward compatible with Xbox One and Xbox Series X|S - see FAQ for more info), and find some friends to play with in matchmaking? There's no specific time for an event - we just want the games back in their prime before they shutdown to custom games only...

WHY YOU SHOULD MAKE IT BEFORE THE SUNSET

Revisit the Good Old Days

Dust off your old copy of Halo 3, and travel back to 2007. Make It Before the Sunset Campaign aims to give new and existing players the authentic early-2010s experience.

GIVE THE ORIGINALS ONE FINAL RUN BEFORE WE MOVE ON

Moving on isn't easy. Whilst more and more players are moving on to the Master Chief Collection, the original releases are slowly being forgotten over time. The originals deserve one last lobby before we put our old discs aside in favour for the Master Chief Collection.

NO XBOX 360? NO PROBLEM.

Your old disc will work on the Xbox One family of consoles* and Xbox Series X|S**. It also means you can replay the classics from anywhere around the world!

*excluding the Xbox One S All-Digital Edition, unless you already own the game digitally.

**excluding the Xbox Series S, unless you already own the game digitally.
